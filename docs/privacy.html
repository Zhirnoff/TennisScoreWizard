Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Tennis Score Wizard is designed as a local Apple Watch tennis scoring app. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

Data Collection

Tennis Score Wizard does not collect personal data from users. The app does not require an account, login, registration, email address, or profile.

Match History and Match Stats

Match history is stored locally on your device. Automatic match stats, including points, serve, return, pressure, game flow, tie-break, and workout summary values, are stored locally as part of your saved match history. We do not receive or access your match history or match stats.

Apple Health and HealthKit

Tennis Score Wizard can optionally request access to Apple Health to record tennis matches as workouts, display live heart rate during a recorded match, and show workout summary values such as average heart rate, max heart rate, and active calories after the workout is saved.

Health Access Is Optional

Health access is optional. If permission is granted, workout and heart rate data are handled by Apple Health on your device. We do not receive, upload, sell, or share Health data.

Network Access

Tennis Score Wizard does not require network access for core functionality. The app does not upload your tennis scores, match history, match stats, workout data, or heart rate data to external servers.

Analytics and Tracking

Tennis Score Wizard does not use third-party analytics, advertising trackers, or tracking technologies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data, match history, match stats, workout data, or heart rate data with third parties.
