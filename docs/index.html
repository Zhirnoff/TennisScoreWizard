Match history

Quick help

How do I score a point?

Tap your side when you win a point. Tap the opponent side when your opponent wins a point.

How does Undo work?

Undo reverses the last scored point and restores the previous match state.

What does the tennis ball mean?

The tennis ball marker shows the serve position for the next point.

How do tie-breaks work?

If tie-break is enabled, a tie-break starts at 6–6 and is played to 7 points, win by 2.

Can I stop a match?

Yes. If you stop a match before it is completed, the current unfinished set is saved in match history.

Can I use it for doubles?

Yes. Select Singles or Doubles before starting the match.

FAQ

Does the app collect data?

No. Match history is stored locally on your device.

Does it require an account?

No. Tennis Score Wizard does not require login or registration.

Can I play without a tie-break?

Yes. Choose No Tie-break during setup.

Can I delete match history?

Yes. Saved matches can be deleted from the history screen.

Privacy

Tennis Score Wizard does not collect personal data.

Match history is stored locally on your device. The app does not require an account and does not transmit, sell, or share personal data.
