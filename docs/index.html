Quick help

How do I score a point?

Tap your side when you win a point. Tap the opponent side when your opponent wins a point.

How does Undo work?

Undo reverses the last scored point and restores the previous match state.

How does the serve marker work?

When you serve, the tennis ball highlights the side you should serve from. When the opponent serves, it highlights the opponent’s serve side from your watch view.

How do tie-breaks work?

If tie-break is enabled, a tie-break starts at 6–6 and is played to 7 points, win by 2.

What happens if I stop a match?

Stopped matches are saved with completed sets and the current unfinished set, so the history keeps the real match context.

Where is my data stored?

Match history is stored locally on your device. The app does not require an account or login.

FAQ

Does the app collect data?

No. Match history is stored locally on your device.

Can I use it for doubles?

Yes. Select Doubles during setup before starting the match.

Can I play without a tie-break?

Yes. Choose No Tie-break during setup to play an advantage set.

Can I delete match history?

Yes. Saved matches can be deleted from the history screen.
